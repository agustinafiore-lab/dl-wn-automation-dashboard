Root cause summary
Delta DL
DL has three distinct failure layers driving the automation drop: (1) Ticketing failures — Multiple Exchange/Issuance and generic ticketing failures chronic all year; (2) Exchange logic — INVALID RV introduced a major spike from Jan 26; (3) Rebook failures — repricing, segment sell, and 118 peaked Dec 25–Mar 26, now improving. DL is the primary driver.
Southwest WN
WN's drop comes from two distinct problems: (1) Inventory step — 866 (no fare found) spiked Apr–May 26, appears to be recovering in Jun; (2) Ticketing step — WN ETKT unused coupons error grown steadily all year, main WN semi-auto failure driver. Lower volume than DL but both need attention.
